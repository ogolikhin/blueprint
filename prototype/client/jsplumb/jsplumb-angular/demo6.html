JsPlumb-Angular Demo

Listening to connections creation:

AngularJS style two way bindings is possible to keep in sync the connection in the UI and the JS model.
Use a 'jsplumb-connections' directive on the root 'jsplumb' element and assign it an array of connections.
You'll have to add a 'model' option to your endpoints options to map your model objects to endpoints.
